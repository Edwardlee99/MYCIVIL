Register Marriage
Important Notice

This register marriage service is for age 21 above only, it will need your following attachment:

  • Both of Couple of Idetification Card
  • Both of Couple of Birth Certificate
  • Both of Photo (Background in Blue Colour or White Colour)
  • Divorce Certificate (IF applicable)
  • Death Certificate (IF applicable)
  • Surat Pengesahan Taraf Perkahwinan yang dikeluarkan oleh Kerajaannya
    • Perlu disahkan oleh pihak perwakilan Malaysia di negara pemohon atau kedutaan negara pemohon di Malaysia DAN
    • Disahkan semula oleh Kementerian Luar Negeri (Wisma Putra) di Putrajaya, Kuching atau Kota Kinabalu.

The attachment can only accept pdf file format and picture or image format.


Please make sure the image or picture format attachment is clear while it is not clear we will fail your approval status!!!


Do not try to choose other than pdf file format and picture or image format to upload attachment. We can fail your approval status too!!!


This Service can be used for register idetification cards at above 21 years old .


All other requirements like above 18 years old until 20 years or under 18 years old cannot be registered on here. Please go to the JPN Office respectively.


If you have a question about register identification cards, you can use our Online Feedback System to send your feedback to the person who is in charge.

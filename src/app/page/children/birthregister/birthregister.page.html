Register Birth Certificate
Important Notice

This register birth certificate service will need your following attachment:

  • Both Parents Idetification Cards
  • Kad Pranatal (Buku Rawatan Mengandung) atau Kad Rawatan Semasa Ibu Mengandung
  • Polis Report when birth in house
  • Borang Pengesahan Kelahiran daripada hospital atau Surat Pengesahan Doktor bagi kelahiran di rumah
  • Surat akuan AM80 sekiranya tiada buku rawatan mengandung atau kad rawatan mengandung.

The attachment can only accept pdf file format and picture or image format.


Please make sure the image or picture format attachment is clear while it is not clear we will fail your approval status!!!


Do not try to choose other than pdf file format and picture or image format to upload attachment. We can fail your approval status too!!!


This Service can be used for register birth certificate at 14 days only.


After 14 days, it will need to pay penalties of RM50 in Sabah and Sarawak while after 60 days for late register in Semenanjung will also need to pay of RM50 .


After 14 days, Semenanjung need to go respectively JPN office to register birth certificate!!!


If you have a question about register birth certificate, you can use our Online Feedback System to send your feedback to the person who is in charge.

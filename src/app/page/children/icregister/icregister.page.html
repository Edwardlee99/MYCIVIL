Register Identification Card
Important Notice

This register identification card is for age 12 to age 16 child service will need your following attachment:

  • One of the Parents Idetification Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Photo (Background in Blue Colour)
  • Supporting Document For Address
    • Bil elektrik atau
    • Bil air atau
    • Bil pembentungan atau
    • Bil utiliti atau
    • Borang taksiran cukai pendapatan atau
    • Perjanjian jual beli rumah atau
    • Perjanjian atau resit sewa rumah atau
    • dokumen sokongan dari Penghulu/ Ketua Kampung/ Tuai Rumah / ADUN / Ahli Parlimen / Majikan/ Angkatan Tentera Malaysia (ATM)
    • atau other

The attachment can only accept pdf file format and picture or image format.


Please make sure the image or picture format attachment is clear while it is not clear we will fail your approval status!!!


Do not try to choose other than pdf file format and picture or image format to upload attachment. We can fail your approval status too!!!


This Service can be used for register idetification cards at between 18 to 16 years old .


Above 16 years old child need to go the respectively JPN office to register Identification Card.


If you have a question about register identification cards, you can use our Online Feedback System to send your feedback to the person who is in charge.
